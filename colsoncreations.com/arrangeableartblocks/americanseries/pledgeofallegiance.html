Pre 1954 Pledge of Allegiance

 

 

Our "Pledge of Allegiance" block carries quite a controversial piece of American history. The pledge was written and published in 1892 by Francis Bellamy and James B. Upham. The original pledge was a little different from the one of today, it first pledged allegiance to "my Flag". In 1923 the words were changed to "the Flag of the United States of America". The words "Under God" were added on June 14th, 1954.
