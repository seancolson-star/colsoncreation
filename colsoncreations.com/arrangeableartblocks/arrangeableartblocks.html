Welcome!

...to Sean Colson's ARRANGEABLE ART BLOCKS

online showroom. Modular concept allows you to choose designs of your liking and arrange them individually to fit desired spaces in your home or office. No two arrangements will be alike!

 

 ARRANGEABLE ART BLOCKS, a unique gypsum cast artwork, 

may be placed in groupings of any size. The blocks are best displayed in wall mounted groupings, and come ready to hang.

Each unique array will reflect your personal touch.

 

You may even have your custom image block from their

own photo or design.  Each block has a certificate on the back

that is signed by the artist.

 

Each three dimensional block measures 6" X 8",

with a depth of 1-1/2".

 

Some 3-D blocks may be hung in either a vertical

or horizontal position.
