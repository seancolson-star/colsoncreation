and grown-ups  relate to life's ups and

downs concerning love.

Sean Colson's

THE BOOK OF LOVE

Sean's illustrated The Book of Love is designed in the same style as his Positive Message Pictures. Featuring lots of hearts and positive thoughts, Sean's illustrated journey starts in the Factory of Love, moving through life's ups and downs of love, through the Junkyard of Broken Hearts, and, in the end, recycles the broken hearts to find love anew.
