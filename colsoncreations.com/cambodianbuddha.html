Cambodian Buddha 

19 ½ " (49.5 cm) Tall, 9" (23 cm) at Widest point, 4 ½" (11.5 cm) at Thickest point.

This historic reproduction is cast in gypsum and gilded by hand in fine brass powder,

giving a real gold look. Each piece is finished with the highest detail and quality.

This is a numbered limited edition,  with a certificate of authenticity included.

 

This statue has an interesting story behind it.

In the early 1990's, I operated my Art Studio in Sarasota, Florida, where I made historic statuary reproductions. There I met an Artist who was on his way

to a Buddhist Monastery to become a Monk.

 

He showed me an original wood carved Buddha which he had found during

his travels in Asia.  He was told the artifact was a 400 year old Cambodian Buddha.

He wanted a few reproductions of his original. In exchange I was able

to make a mold, and he granted me the rights to reproduce it.

I hope you enjoy this unique treasure

which will bring peace and harmony to your home.
