These POSITIVE MESSAGE limited edition prints are the original works of artist SEAN COLSON. Sean's expression of happiness is found in this special style he has created of positive messages. These prints of Sean's paintings are designed to enlighten you, and fill your home full of Love, Peace, Joy, and Happiness!

 

Each numbered limited edition print measures 3" to 4" wide, by 5" to 5 ½" long. Printed with archival ink on 100% acid free natural cotton bond paper. Mounted on a 5 ¾" x 7 ¾" 100% acid free matt board background. Ready to frame as a picture, or display as a card!
