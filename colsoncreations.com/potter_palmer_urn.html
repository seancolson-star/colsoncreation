Sarasota's Historic Spanish Point Urn

 

The "Mrs. Potter Palmer Urn" from Historic Spanish Point.

Own this stunning historic reproduction from Spanish Point. Mrs. Potter Palmer wanted statuary on her estate but didn't have any molds, but she did have the mold for the base of the balusters (columns at the entrance) used in the construction of her home. So she ingeniously had them cast just the balusters bases in a Terracotta color and turn them upside down for planters. This is why when you look at the ornate flower & grape patterns on the side of the urn, they are upside down (going up, instead of down). Which gives this urn a great character, unique to Mrs. Potter Palmer's style and her early days as one of the original settlers in Sarasota in the early 1900's.

 

26" Wide X 22" High, Approximately 250 lbs
